## 🎨 Design System Summary

### Typography

- **Display:** Spectral serif (headlines, numbers, quotes)
- **Body:** Inter sans-serif (paragraphs, UI)
- **Hierarchy:** h1 (2.5-4rem) → h2 (1.75-2.25rem) → h3 (1.125rem) → body (0.9375-1.0625rem)

### Colors

- **Accent:** Deep Forest Green (#14532D)
- **Backgrounds:** White (#FFFFFF) + Light gray surface
- **Text:** ink (primary) → ink-2 (secondary) → ink-3 (tertiary)

### Spacing

- Sections: `clamp(5rem, 10vw, 8rem)`
- Subsections: `clamp(3rem, 6vw, 5rem)`
- Cards: `clamp(1.5rem, 3vw, 2rem)`
- Large gaps: `clamp(4rem, 8vw, 6rem)`

### Components

- **Cards:** White on surface, 12px radius, 1px border, hover effects
- **Icons:** 48px squares with accent background, rounded 12px
- **Tag badges:** 0.6875rem uppercase, accent-bg, rounded 6px
- **Number circles:** 32-44px, accent background, white text
- **Tables:** Alternating rows, proper padding, color-coded content
- **FAQ Accordion:** Max-width 900px, smooth animations
- **CTA Banner:** Accent green background, white text, 2 CTAs
